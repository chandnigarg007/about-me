Often confused looks

Industrial and rustic
Every industrial look is rustic but not every rustic look is industrial.
Eclectic and modern
Every eclectic look is modern but not every modern look is eclectic.
Victorian and shabby chic
Shabby chic is a more feminine version of victorian. Think of it like this- the difference between the two is that of a teenager and an adult, both who adore glamour and luxury. The teenager princess will desire for a more girly and sensational look whereas the adult will long for class and royalty, a little more mature rather than sensible.
Modern and minimalist
Modern is more stylized and has a classier look. It may have less clutter but that look has a purpose - when there are minimal amounts of furniture, it is a look created in the room, whereas in a minimalistic scheme, it serves as a function rather than a statement.
